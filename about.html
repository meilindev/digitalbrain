👋 I’m Mimi — an undergraduate candidate in Political Science at King's College London . Tech is an interest for me/how it overlaps with politics/wellbeing/autism//edit/ My research explores how gentle, personal web aesthetics can support wellbeing, digital slowness, and inclusive online communities.

Before academia, I worked as a front‑end developer and design systems lead. I believe that soft design — thoughtful typography, restrained colour palettes, and warm micro‑interactions — is not frivolous; it’s a form of care. I’m also a big fan of matcha, stationery, and my cat Mochi. 🐈‍⬛
